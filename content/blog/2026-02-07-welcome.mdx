I built this portfolio to answer a simple question:

> Can you ship, operate, and explain systems clearly?

Projects show outcomes. A blog shows *how you think*.

## What You’ll See Here

- Build notes: tradeoffs, constraints, and the boring-but-important details
- DevOps and platform engineering: Kubernetes, GitOps, observability, CI/CD
- Frontend engineering: performance, accessibility, UI architecture

## The Rule

Every post ends with something you can re-use: a checklist, a snippet, or a pattern.
