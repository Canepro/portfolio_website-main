GitOps is less about tools and more about **control loops**.

## The Mental Model

1. Desired state lives in Git.
2. A reconciler (like Argo CD) continuously compares desired vs actual.
3. Drift becomes visible and correctable.

## Practical Checklist

- Keep "platform" and "apps" separate (different repos or at least different paths).
- Make rollbacks boring (one commit, one sync).
- Treat access like production: auth, audit, and least privilege.
